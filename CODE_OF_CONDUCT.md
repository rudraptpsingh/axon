# Code of Conduct

Axon is built for a technical community. Keep discussion direct, respectful,
and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and specific.
- Assume good intent, but correct mistakes clearly.
- Critique code, tests, and product decisions, not people.
- Help new contributors understand the privacy and local-runtime goals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private machine data, logs, or credentials without consent.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation
for behavior that harms the project or community.
